* About Us *
Established in 2000, the Harvard College Piano Society (HCPS) is dedicated to fostering piano performance, pedagogy, and appreciation both within and beyond the Harvard community. It is devoted to forging a more intimate and united community of musicians, strengthening the ties between Harvard and local conservatories, and expanding student concert opportunities. HCPS conducts master classes, workshops, and recital series with pianists from around the world. We welcome both amateur and professional pianists, ane even non-musicians who just love music are welcome! We hold regular meetings and social events, so come join us! We would love to see you!
World-renowned Pianist Yuja Wang with Former President George Ko '16
